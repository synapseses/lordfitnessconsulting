How can Exercise Physiology help you?

Accredited Exercise Physiologist apply and prescribe Exercise as the intervention to help manage or prevent a host of health conditions, including Diabetes, Osteopenia and Osteoporosis.

Any condition that exercise is proven to help, an Exercise Physiologist can assist. This can also include cardiovascular and neurological conditions all the way through to Mental health disorders and musculoskeletal injuries.
